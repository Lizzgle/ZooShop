Our story began in the very center of Minsk. In 2003, our first pet store appeared at the Komarovsky market, the huge noisy heart of the city. There were few suppliers at that time, so no one had previously offered such a wide selection of feeds, toys, and care products in one place. Our showcase has become a real "zoo bazaar" for animal lovers. The first Zoobazar store was quickly followed by the second, third, and tenth. We decided to take the business to a new level, and we succeeded.

In 2007, we received a veterinary license and began offering not only the largest selection of pet food and accessories in the city, but also a line of in-demand veterinary drugs.

Zoobazar continued to develop and began to appear outside of Minsk. The first regional store opened in Baranovichi in 2008, followed by other cities of the country. By the end of 2016, the chain had 36 points of sale, and the Zoobazar sign became recognizable by many buyers.

In 2019, the Zoobazar network opens the 100th facility. And this is a new chapter, each line of which begins with the word "first". The first pet store in the industry with the Cash and carry format, where animal lovers have the opportunity to make small wholesale purchases. The first contact center in the pet business to support customers and employees: we want to always be there and provide high-quality information support.

As of June 2023, we are working in 50 cities, including all regional centers of the country. Zoobazar has more than 300 points of sale of various formats: from mini-markets to self-service stores. All pet stores meet standards aimed at maximizing customer satisfaction. The assortment of each of them includes more than 6,500 items of goods from foreign and domestic manufacturers, allowing customers to make a choice of feed or related products in any of the price segments. The quality of the products is confirmed by certificates and hygiene certificates. We promptly respond to market changes and provide our customers with fair and favorable prices. The biggest pride of Zoobazar is more than 200,000 of our active customers who make over 500,000 purchases every month. This is the main proof that we are developing in the right direction.
